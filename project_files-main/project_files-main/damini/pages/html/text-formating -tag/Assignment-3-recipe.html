Delicious Veg Biryani Recipe

This Veg Biryani is a flavorful and easy to makedish that brings joy to every meal.Perfect for weekends!
It's rich in Proteinand Vitamin B12and testes best when served hot.


Ingredients

  • 2 cup Basmati Rice
  • 1 cup Mixed vegetables
  • 1 Onion(sliced)
  • 1 tsp Biryani Masala
  • Salt, oil,Mint,Coriander

How to Cook

  1. wash and soak rice for 30 minutes
  2. Saute onion and vegetables
  3. Add spices and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Mix with cooked rice and simmer
  5. Serve hot with raita
