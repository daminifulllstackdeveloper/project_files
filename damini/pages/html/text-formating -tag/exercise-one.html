AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is technology enabling machines to perform tasks requiring human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making

By processing vast amounts of data, AI systems identify patterns, recognize objects, understand language, and adapt their behavior over time. AI is used in many areas, from virtual assistants and self-driving cars to medical image analysis and customer service applications, improving efficiency and accuracy in countless industries.

AVATAR

Avatar is a 2009 science fiction film directed by James Cameron about a paraplegic Marine, Jake Sully who is sent to the moon Pandora to explore and exploit its resources, clashing with the indigenous Na'vi people in a conflict over their home. The film stars Sam Worthington and Zoe Saldana, was released in 2009, and features revolutionary visual effects. It was a major box office success and spawned the Avatar franchise, including thesequel Avatar: The way of water.

Key Facts:
  • Release Date: December 18, 2009
  • Genre: Epic Science Fiction
  • Production: Produced by 20th Century Fox
  • Success: The film was a global box office success, becoming one of the highest-grossing movies of all time.
  • Sequels: Due to its success, Cameron signed a deal to direct four sequels, the first of which, Avatar: The Way of Water, was released in 2022.

THE HOUSE NEXT DOOR

"The House Next Door" can refer to a 2017 Indian horror film or a 1978 horror novel and its 2006 television movie adaptation.

The House Next Door (2017 Indian film)
  • Original titles: The film was released in three languages simultaneously: as Aval in Tamil, Gruham in Telugu, and The House Next Door in Hindi.
  • Plot: A newlywed couple moves into a home in the Himalayas and soon learns that the family next door, specifically their teenage daughter, is haunted by a malevolent spirit. The husband, a neurosurgeon, must eventually turn to an exorcism to help save his new neighbors.
  • Inspiration: The film is reportedly based on true events.
  • Cast: The film stars Siddharth and Andrea Jeremiah as the married couple, with Atul Kulkarni as the neighbor and Anisha Victor as his daughter.
The House Next Door (1978 novel and 2006 TV movie)
  • Original work: The 2006 television movie is based on the 1978 bestseller novel by Anne Rivers Siddons.
  • Plot: The story follows a happily married couple, Col and Walker Kennedy, whose peaceful life is shattered when a beautiful and seemingly normal house is built next door. The couple slowly realizes that a terrible, evil force resides in the house and begins to terrorize its residents.
  • 2006 movie: The Lifetime TV movie adaptation stars Lara Flynn Boyle as Col and Mark-Paul Gosselaar as the architect who builds the house. It was noted for a significant change to the ending from the original novel.
